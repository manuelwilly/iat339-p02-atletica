About Us

Company Profile

Atletica provides a full range of football industry products to consumers at unbeatable prices. Our online store ships to 112 countries using the most efficient transportation methods available in that country. Aside from consumer retail, we also cater to our customers in the form of club sales, group sales, and local sales with special pricing tiers.

Our Mission

Founded in 2007, Atletica has committed to provide the cheapest possible pricing to football fans across the globe. Years later, we are now a 100 person company with partnerships and distribution networks across the world. We strive to provide even more comprehensive lineup of football products.

Our Guarantee

We ship our products all around the world. If you happen to find issues with the product you've purchased, don't worry, we've got you covered. All products sold through Atletica are covered by 1 year warranty and DOA protection.

Leadership Team
