Product Review

“Second greatest boots I have ever bought!”

First of all, the "plasma" color is basically bright pink. It looks more red in the pictures to me, but it is definitely bright pink. Since they are soccer boots I'm okay with that because they still look cool, but just an FYI. "Peacoat" is dark navy.

They fit well. My foot is a little wide but the width of these is just right. I have used other puma boots that fit much more narrow even when I size a half size up. The EvoPOWER line is more suitable for a wide foot as compared to the EvoSPEED line. I think I would have preferred leather, but the synthetic upper seems pretty durable and flexible and doesn't feel thick or heavy.

Submitted by Aaron / Puma evoPOWER 3.2 FG Youths
